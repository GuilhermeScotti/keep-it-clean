Why should we keep the streets clean?

Keeping our streets clean not only enhances the beauty and safety of our neighborhoods but also promotes a healthier environment by reducing pollution and encouraging community pride. Cleaner streets contribute to better public health, support local wildlife, and foster a more positive and inviting atmosphere for everyone.
